About the Game

Super Tiles

is a fresh and fast-paced tile-matching game that challenges your brain and your reflexes. Slide colorful tiles into the perfect spot, create powerful combos, and clear the board in record time! Whether you're a casual player or a puzzle pro, Super Tiles will keep your mind sharp and your fingers busy with satisfying gameplay and endless levels to master. Core Features

  • Fast-Paced Tile Matching

    Slide and combine identical tiles to create powerful chains and clear levels.

  • Boosters & Power-Ups

    Use lightning strikes, color bombs, and tile shufflers to escape tricky puzzles.

  • Dynamic Levels

    No two boards are the same! Enjoy ever-changing patterns and increasing difficulty.

  • Vibrant Visuals

    Eye-catching colors and smooth animations that make every move feel satisfying.

  • Offline Mode

    Play anytime, anywhere—no internet required!

  • Stress-Free Fun

    Designed for both quick breaks and long relaxing sessions.

How to play

Slide tiles into empty spots on the board.

Match 3 or more identical tiles in a line or group.

Advance through levels to unlock new challenges.

Trigger combos and clear the board for high scores.

Use boosters wisely when things get tight!
